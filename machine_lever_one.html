Lab Workspace (Ideal Lever)
Mechanical Advantage (MA)
MA = Load ÷ Effort
MA = Effort Arm ÷ Load Arm
MA = 8.0 ÷ 2.0 = 4.00
Velocity Ratio (VR)
VR = Displacement of Effort ÷ Displacement of Load
VR = sE ÷ sL = 8.0 ÷ 2.0 = 4.00
Required Effort (E)
E = L ÷ MA = 200 ÷ 4.00 = 50.0 N
